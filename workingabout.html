Maleni Palacios is an emerging researcher and author passionate about monetary policy, fintech, gender equity and applying innovation and entrepreneurship onto inclusive strategy building. She graduated from Barnard College, Columbia University with a BA in economics and is an Athena Center for Leadership alum. She is the founder of The Pa'lante Podcast; a podcast on the first-generation college experience. Currently, she is an Associate at Capco.
