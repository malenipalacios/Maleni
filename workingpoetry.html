The sun, the moon, and I will rise.

Beyond your eyes

4 words, utility, a bed, a window - you
your thoughts and dreams and questions
circle and leave the room, however,
you remain still

the open outside, the ground, the air - you
your anxiety, worries, and hesitations
leave just us showing - they arrived
and you still remain

what's in your heads is not all that is
the actions, the movements, are
and also make you
utility is an embodiment of all

A B C D ...

My ground and teacher, the one who taught me everything
Culture, authenticity, humbleness, charm and coolness
Intellect and the storytelling of real pain and perseverance.
Ours is a story of transcendence, the development of a leader

My soul and complement, everything that I spoke to the universe of
An artist, gifted with words and talent, a real person, sharp and mine
Distinct but too grounding and the perfect match
Ours is a story of partnership and eternal soul

My ocean, who’s waters give me all the love that will forever give
The grace, the calm and collected, the lady and best humanizer
Down to the seabed and up to the sun
Ours is a story of unconditional love, so much of it, that I now give from our well.

My moon, amidst it all there lies compassion, respect, a deep love
An appreciation for the other, an understanding, a hunger for the new and old
Beauty beyond me, gumption and character, a unique grace
Ours is a story of companionship, familial links that can never be broken

My one and only. Constants in my life.
Iterating, humanizing, cultivating, making anew and afresh
All the love that I hold for my abc’s…
Giving me this magical carpet ride.

Here is home

Sometimes growing up hurts.
Let’s honor slowness.
Make peace with time
Make a generous offering

We are an integral whole.
We. As in everything on the Earth.
And with that
You can’t be free of pain if others are in pain

Always opening new doors.
We rise by lifting others.
We rise ...
We rise ...

Ceteris Paribus

‘The elementary basics’ they cry out
‘yes I agree’, but how do you maintain the basics
when so much rhetoric about the complex system is taking place

how do you reconcile both? That is the question of our time
the question of our time that some know the answer to
but refuse to share for lack of a cut

that’s the paradox. I don’t know if I should feel sad for them
or what message I should convey to the others
for after all, it’s not all harmonious as it is perceived

so what then is the answer, does it just flow, is it a part of time
rendering all of its implications and just following suit
feelings of care, of betterment, or prosperity all get jumbled up in my head

yet here we are barely a quarter in, three more to go
much time to begin to understand it, much time to talk to others
about the elementary basics.

The Antithesis

It could have been worse
It could be worse
So what’s the trouble mama?
Perspective, it’s all about perspective

Focus your gaze, do what you can,
And hope for the best.
It could be worse, much worse.
Living, but you are

If you’re not dying your living.
It’s all about perspective.
In the meantime, give me your art
Give me your story,

Give me a piece of which I, we
Can remember you by, that is
Every person’s gift, to the world
No one can take that away from you

So give me your art, because it could have
Been worse. But it is right now, take a breath,
And let’s keep it moving mama?
Give me your 5 minutes.

the truth is, it never is
